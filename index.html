Game Instructions

Game board consists of sixteen "cards" arranged in a grid. Deck is made up of eight different pairs of cards, each with different symbols on one side. Cards with symbols behind them are positioned randomly on the grid.

Rules are very simple: Flip over two hidden cards at a time to locate the ones that match!
